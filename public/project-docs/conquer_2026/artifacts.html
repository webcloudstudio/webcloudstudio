Artifacts

Artifacts are rare items that provide passive bonuses to the nation that holds them. They spawn on the map at world generation or appear via the Ancient Ruins world event. Some are guarded by monster units.

Artifact Hunters receive double benefit from all artifacts and can passively detect nearby artifacts on the map.

Artifact Rules

  • Artifacts are visible on the map only when a scout enters the sector
  • Some are guarded by monster armies that must be defeated first
  • When an army carrying artifacts is defeated, artifacts transfer to the victor
  • A nation can hold multiple artifacts (effects stack)
  • Artifact Hunters: hold double the stated effect + passively detect nearby artifacts
