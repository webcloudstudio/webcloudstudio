I'm focused on AI-assisted software development and building repeatable, scalable workflows. I think about development the same way I've scaled data pipelines: with end-to-end repeatable processes, solid fundamentals, and a relentless focus on quality.

I've been a gamer my entire life—it shaped how I think about complex systems and persistence. I killed dragons and finished the Plane of Time in Everquest back when that actually meant something. That same love of building and mastering complex systems drives what I do with technology.

I write code, architect platforms, and help teams think through hard problems. I'm also a published author on database administration and agile practices. If you're thinking about scaling your team or systems, let's talk.
