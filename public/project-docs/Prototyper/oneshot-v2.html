Agile Team Oneshot Build Model

An Agile build mode for projects that are small in scope but large in uncertainty. The LLM runs your development team and implements your vision; you are the product owner — you submit features, answer questions, and review working demos through a custom Per‑Project Collaboration Console. Spikes investigate the unknowns, stories deliver the known work against acceptance criteria, and every confirmed decision is reconciled back into the specification.

Core Design Ideas

💡
The Developer acts as the Agile Product OwnerAgile. The LLM acts as the development team Output must include demos and evidence at each state with constant quality reviews
💡
The LLM divides up the specification and creates user stories and spikes Spikes and Stores relate and inform each other
💡
Custom web console for demos and evidence Why Not! Evidence is presents at each stage - enabling review and workflow - built in demos for each story and spike
💡
Every spike and decision leaves an artifact. Investigations and choices are written down, then read back into the build, so nothing is lost between steps.
💡
Development team expected to act in full test driven development agile mode Enables new acceptance criteria as feedback for iteration
💡
Iterate oneshot as the answers firm up. AC and Review Notes and Pre-Known workflow enable minimal changes project iteration.
💡
Questions and Design Decisions The LLM writes questions into sections in their output markdown
